# Downloads Folder

## Instructions

Place your lead magnet PDF file here:

1. Name it: `growth-readiness-checklist.pdf`
2. Upload it to this folder

The website is configured to download this file when users click:
- "Download Free Checklist" button on the hero section
- "Download Free Checklist" button on the final CTA section

## What to include in your checklist

Consider creating a 1-2 page PDF with:
- 10 growth red flags to watch for
- Growth readiness scorecard
- Quick self-assessment questions
- Your DP5 branding and contact info

This captures leads who aren't ready to schedule a call but are interested in your expertise.
